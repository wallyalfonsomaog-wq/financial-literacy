Zero-Based Budgeting

What's Zero-Based Budgeting?

Zero-based budgeting means assigning every peso you earn to a specific purpose so that nothing is left unplanned. It doesn’t mean you spend all your money each month, but rather that you allocate it carefully to categories like bills, savings, or future expenses. This method ensures your income is fully accounted for, helping you stay disciplined and avoid waste. By giving each peso a job, you gain clarity and control over your finances.

  • Rent, utilities, and groceries
  • Transportation and fuel costs
  • Emergency savings fund
  • Retirement contributions
  • Future expenses like car repairs, vacations, or children’s education
  • Debt repayment (credit cards, loans)
  • Personal spending categories (entertainment, hobbies)
