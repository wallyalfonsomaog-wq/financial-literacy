50/30/20 Method

What's 50/30/20?

The 50/30/20 budget rule is a simple way to manage your money by dividing income into three clear categories. It ensures that essentials are covered, allows room for personal enjoyment, and builds long-term financial security. This method is easy to follow and helps prevent overspending while encouraging savings.

  • 50% Needs: Rent or mortgage, utilities, groceries, transportation, healthcare
  • 30% Wants: Dining out, entertainment, hobbies, vacations, shopping for extras
  • 20% Savings/Debt Repayment: Emergency fund, retirement savings, investments, paying off credit cards or loans
